Thesis

Sports media is broken. The numbers belong to the fans now.

For 30 years, NFL analytics lived in three walled gardens: ESPN graphics, league press releases, and gambling shows. Modern fans want what front offices have — a transparent model they can interrogate, save, and share. That product doesn't exist yet. We're building it.

01

The market is already paying

Fantasy platforms, betting apps, and subscription analytics products have proven that fans will pay $9–$30/mo for an edge. None of them are transparent, fan-first, or built for how modern fans actually share.

02

Modern tooling changed the economics

A focused six-person team now ships what took 50 people in 2018. The barrier to a defensible sports product collapsed. The winner is whoever builds the brand and the data flywheel first.

03

Distribution is solved

Group chats, Reddit, TikTok, and Discord are the new sports cable. Exportable charts and one-line "model says" receipts spread the brand for free. We're built for that loop.
