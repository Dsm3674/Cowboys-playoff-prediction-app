★ Origin

Where the idea began.

A playoff-odds calculator became a transparent NFL analytics lab for fans, classrooms, and curious builders.

Cowboys IQ started with the question every fan keeps asking: what are the real chances?

The answer became a model: Monte Carlo simulation, live win probability, Clutch Index, scenario paths, and player-impact scoring without the hot-take fog.

The project is open source. Fork it, improve it, use it in class, or help turn it into a sharper sports analytics product.
