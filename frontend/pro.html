Inside the Quantum Engine

100,000 simulations. One season. Every Sunday.

Pro unlocks the full output, not just the topline percentage. Here's what you actually see.

01

Full distribution view

Don't just see "67% playoff odds." See the full simulation histogram — including how often Dallas misses, gets a wild card, wins the division, or earns the 1-seed.

02

Chaos parameter control

Slide the chaos dial. See how playoff odds respond when the model assumes more variance (injuries, weather, upsets) vs. less. Pro users get the full 0–1.0 range.

03

Game-by-game leverage scores

Every remaining game gets a "leverage" number — how much the playoff probability moves if Dallas wins vs. loses. The most important games aren't always the ones on national TV.

04

Seed-by-seed pathing

The exact win-loss combinations needed to earn the 1, 2, 3, or wild card seeds. Pro shows the probability of each path, not just the existence of it.

05

Player-impact decay curves

How much does the Cowboys' playoff probability decline per game a key player misses? See it as a curve, not a guess.

06

Model assumption transparency

Every Pro view shows its inputs: which Elo ratings, which strength-of-schedule weights, which injury adjustments. You're never trusting a black box.
